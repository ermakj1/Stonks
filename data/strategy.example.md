# Trading Strategy

## Goals
- Build long-term wealth through a diversified portfolio
- Maintain core positions in quality companies
- Use options only for defined-risk strategies

## Risk Tolerance
- Comfortable holding through 20-30% drawdowns
- No naked shorts or unlimited-risk positions
- Maximum single-position size: 25% of portfolio

## Rebalancing Rules
- Rebalance quarterly or when any position drifts >5% from target
- Take partial profits when a position gains >50% from cost basis

## Options Strategy
- Buy calls only on stocks I'd be happy owning at the strike price
- Keep options exposure under 5% of total portfolio value
